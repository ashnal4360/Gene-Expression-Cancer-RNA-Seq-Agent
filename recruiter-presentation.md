# Recruiter Presentation

## What is is
An interactive bioinformatics app that compares cancer gene-expression profiles and surfaces the most differentially expressed genes.

## Problem
Researchers and students need a fast way to inspect large RNA-seq datasets and identify candidate genes for deeper study.

## Solution
This app automates file loading, group selection, statistical comparison, and result ranking.

## Why it stands out
- Bioinformatics focus.
- Agent-like analysis flow.
- Real dataset.
- Interactive visualization.
- Downloadable outputs.
